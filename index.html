Who we are

Soter Labs is the trusted GovOps provider building the Ozone Executor Agent, and now operates the Amatsu Executor Agent. We design and manage the scalable frameworks and processes that ensure the compliant, transparent, and accountable operation of Sky's Agents.

In ancient Greek mythology, Soter was the personified spirit of safety, preservation, and deliverance from harm. We carry that name because protecting what matters — through reliable operations, transparent processes, and accountable execution — is at the heart of everything we do.

Our goal is to execute the Sky's mandate — scaling the ecosystem from 10B to 1T USDS — by providing reliable, world-class operational services for its advanced DeFi primitives.
